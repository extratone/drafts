AppWish App Store Review

I’ve been waiting over ten years for this app

As someone who writes about iOS and iOS apps, I’ve been looking for this particular app for as long as I can remember. Especially thoughtful functions are the import and export tools, which made absurdly quick work of intelligently disseminating the various app lists I’d maintained in a variety of formats.

Within seconds, an issue I’ve been contending with forever was completely and comprehensively solved. BLESS.
