How to Include Rich Text Links in Media Captions on Telegram for iOS

  • How to Include Rich Text Links in Media Captions on Telegram for iOS · Issue #6 · extratone/t
  • YouTube Short
  • Telegram Message
  1. Assuming you have Rich Text in your clipboard, paste and send it to Saved Messages.
  2. Hold the resulting message until the context menu appears, tap “Copy.”
  3. Paste the result in a message, then add your media.
