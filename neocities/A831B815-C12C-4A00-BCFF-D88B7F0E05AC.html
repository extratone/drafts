Others I’ve tried:

  • Amaroq: Hasn’t been meaningfully updated in ages.
  • Mast: So much promise, but the author is also writing the popular new Aviary app fo Twitter and seems to be concentrating on that.
  • Mercury for Mastodon: I love it, but iPhone only. It operates in “zoomed in to fill the screen” mode on iPad and that ruins it for me.

Honorable mention:

  • Linky: Not for reading Mastodon, but brilliant for posting to it.
