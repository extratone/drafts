Siri Speech Synthesis (MacOS)

Creates an audio file of text - either from the clipboard, or from the services menu - bypassing the broken Safari Reader action on MacOS

About

At the moment, the Safari Reader action upon which my iOS-borned Siri Speech Synthesis Shortcutdepends is not functioning on MacOS, so I’ve created this (hopefully temporary) solution that performs the same function, though it will require you to select and/or copy the body of an article to the clipboard yourself.
