## Copy a collection from LookUp to your Clipboard.

![LookUpIcon](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/43663476/153735758-977f73c0-b151-45af-8d8b-1404dcb67401.png)

This shortcut copies an alphabetized list of terms with their definitions from a [LookUp](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/lookup-english-dictionary/id872564448) collection of your specification to the clipboard.
